Crazy Mary

Posted on Febraury 05, 2021

Crazy Mary had a Little lamb
It's fleece was dark as coal.
Everywhere that crazy Mary went
The lamb was sure to go.

Crazy Mary had a long chain
that she tied her little lamb with
She would drag it all around town
Coercing it with a whip.

Crazy Mary was a disturbed little girl
Everyone in town knew
Crazy Mary had no friends
Like a tree planted by the River side,
her sadness grew.

Crazy Mary had a Little lamb
It's fleece was white as snow
Crazy Mary bleached and dyed it's fleece
Until it became as dark as coal.
