Sail the seas

Posted on Febraury 05, 2021

Some sail the seas
of temporary forgetfulness
With a bottle of whiskey or wine.
I would rather sail those dead seas
with a bottle of amnesia
brewed to perfection.
I will raise my glass in the air,
knowing that I will forget you
as soon as the drink dances
through the walls of my throat
