The way you stare

Posted on Febraury 05, 2021

I see the way you look at me
Your eyes,
I see the way you stare.

Your lips
Cold and wanting
Begging to part mine open
as Moses did part the Red Sea.

My touch
I feel how it weakens your knees
And turns you as liquid as
butter on a hot stove.
Some call it love,
Others call it obsession,
I call it Power.
