About Me

Posted on December 30, 2020

My name is Karen Efe, I have a funny-sounding middle name which we might have trouble pronouncing. I enjoy writing, painting, reading,singing, travelling, going to the beach (I have never gone to the beach, but I am certain that I would enjoy it), making videos,dancing with my friends and family (I am an absolutely terrible dancer).

A part of me feels like I was born for the Arts, but I found myself drawn to the Sciences(I am currently studying Medicine and Surgery at the University of Port-Hacourt, Nigeria) and I am trying to strike a balance between these two since they individually find a way to pull me closer to them.

I'm also a lover of animals (animals that do not crawl and animals that are not scary - rats and cockroaches do not belong here obviously)
